Student Performance Analysis Report
==================================================
Key Findings:
1. There is a positive correlation between study time and grades.
2. Family support significantly influences student performance.
3. Students who participate in extracurricular activities tend to have better grades.

Recommendations:
1. Encourage students to allocate more study time to improve grades.
2. Implement programs to enhance family engagement in students' education.
3. Provide resources for students to balance extracurricular activities with academic responsibilities.
